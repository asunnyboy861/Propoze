Managing Your Subscription

You can manage or cancel your Propoze Pro subscription at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find Propoze and tap it
  5. Choose to cancel or change your subscription

Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Restore Purchases

If you've purchased Pro but don't see your features:

  1. Open Propoze
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Upgrade to Pro
  4. Tap Restore Purchases

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a proposal?
Tap the + button on the Proposals tab, choose a template, and start editing.

Can I add my company logo?
Yes! Go to Settings → Brand Settings to customize your proposals with your company name, tagline, and colors.

How do I add an e-signature?
While editing a proposal, tap the Sign button in the toolbar to add your signature using Apple Pencil or your finger.
